--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.3 On-Device Throw / Form Analysis (Apple Vision)

DiscVibe includes an optional “Form Check” / throw analysis feature that helps you review your throwing form.

What data is processed
• Only the short video you choose from your Photo Library for this feature
• The video is used solely to estimate body-pose / movement feedback (for example, arm and shoulder angles)

Who processes the data
• Processing is performed only on your device
• We use Apple’s on-device Vision framework (including human body pose detection)
• DiscVibe does not send this video to DiscVibe servers for AI analysis
• DiscVibe does not share this video with any third-party AI service or remote AI API

What we do NOT do
• We do not upload your selected throw video to any cloud AI provider
• We do not sell or share this video with advertisers or third-party AI companies
• Temporary local copies may be kept briefly in the app sandbox only for processing and are not used for other purposes

Your control
• This feature runs only after you choose a video and agree to the in-app notice
• You can decline the notice and continue using other parts of the app
• You can control Photo Library access in iOS Settings
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
